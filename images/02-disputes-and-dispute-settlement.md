# Figure briefs — Chapter 2: Disputes

## Figure 2.1: Dispute-resolution funnel
**Placement:** chapter opening.
**Description:** Funnel with four layers — negotiation (largest), mediation, arbitration, litigation (smallest). Arrows showing filtering at each stage.
**Pedagogical purpose:** Litigation is the exception; most disputes resolve at lower levels.
**Style:** Plain funnel, serif.

## Figure 2.2: Thomas-Kilmann Conflict Modes
**Placement:** Concept 1.
**Description:** 2x2 matrix with assertiveness on x-axis, cooperativeness on y-axis. Five modes plotted: forcing (high assertiveness/low cooperativeness), yielding (low/high), avoiding (low/low), compromising (mid/mid), collaborating (high/high).
**Pedagogical purpose:** No mode is correct in all situations.
**Style:** 2x2 grid, plain serif.
