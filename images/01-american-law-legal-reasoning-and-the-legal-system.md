# Figure briefs — Chapter 1: American Law

## Figure 1.1: Four sources of American law
**Placement:** chapter opening.
**Description:** Four columns: Constitutional, Statutory, Regulatory, Judicial. Arrows showing interaction (Constitution constrains all; statutes implement; regulations operationalize; judicial interprets).
**Pedagogical purpose:** No single source operates alone.
**Style:** Plain serif diagram, no color.

**Placement:** Concept 2.

**Pedagogical purpose:** Hierarchical authorization.
**Style:** Flowchart, plain serif.
