# Business Law: with LLMs
## Compact Tik TOC Architecture

**Book type:** MBA / business course textbook  
**Status:** Retrospective architecture for developed content  

## Core Thesis

This book teaches business law as a practical risk and responsibility framework: contracts, organizations, employment, IP, torts, regulation, and dispute prevention.

## Primary Reader

MBA students, business undergraduates, founders, and managers who need legal literacy but are not training to become lawyers.

## Learning Arc

1. **Legal foundations:** sources of law, courts, contracts, liability, and organizations.
2. **Business operations:** employment, agency, property, IP, sales, finance, and compliance.
3. **Strategic risk:** disputes, governance, ethics, data, AI, and international issues.

## Chapter Architecture

Each chapter should start with a business scenario, identify the legal issue, explain the rule, and show the managerial decision.

## Terminal Capability

The reader can spot common legal issues, ask better counsel questions, and avoid preventable business-law mistakes.

## Adoption Risks

- Must avoid giving legal advice.
- Law changes by jurisdiction and time.
- AI-generated legal summaries require caution and source verification.
