## About the Author

**Nik Bear Brown** teaches at Northeastern University, where his MBA-track and graduate students regularly navigate the legal landscape of AI startups, IP claims, and platform liability. Through [Bear Brown & Company](https://www.bearbrown.co/), he advises early-stage AI startups; the law of the field is part of the practical work of getting a real prototype to investors.

His PhD in Computer Science is from UCLA. He is the founder of [Humanitarians AI](https://www.humanitarians.ai/), the architect of the **Brutalist** AI-assisted production system, and the author of the *with LLMs* textbook series.

He works in Boston. [nikbearbrown.com](https://www.nikbearbrown.com) · [irreducibly.xyz](https://irreducibly.xyz) · [skepticism.ai](https://www.skepticism.ai)
